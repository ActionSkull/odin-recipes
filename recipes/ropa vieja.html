Ropa Vieja

Cuba's national dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds flank steak, skirt steak, or brisket
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 medium onions, cut into thick slices
  • 1 large red bell pepper, cut into thick slices
  • 1 large green bell pepper, cut into thick slices
  • 3 large cloves garlic, cut into thin slices
  • 1 jalapeno (ribs and seeds removed for less heat, if desired), cut into thin slices
  • 2 teaspoons of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of oregano
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 can (28 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • Â½ cup green olives
  • 1 tablespoon capers, drained
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper

Steps

  1. Add everything to a 5-6 quart crockpot, with the meat on the bottom. Cover and cook on high until very tender, 5 hours (or on low 10 hours).
  2. Stir with a fork or other utensil to break up the beef into shreds. Serve with rice.
