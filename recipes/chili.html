Chili

This is my go-to, quick recipe for chili with beans

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp. Olive Oil
  • 2 Medium Onions, chopped
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, chopped
  • 2 Lbs. Beef or Pork
  • 2 8oz Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Cups of Water or Beer
  • 2 Tsp. Salt
  • 2 Tsp. Ground Cumin
  • 1 Tbsp. Oregano
  • 4 Tbsp. Chili Powder
  • 2 Tsp. Paprika
  • 2 Tsp. Cocoa Powder
  • ½ Tsp. Cayenne Red Pepper
  • 2 Cans Beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2-3 Tbsp. Masa Harina or Flour, dissolved in ¼ cup of water

Steps

  1. Sauté the onions and peppers in the olive oil. In a separate pan, brown the beef.
  2. Drain the fat from the beef, and add the beef to the onions.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients except for the flour.
  4. Simmer for 1 hour, and then slowly stir in the flour mixture until desired consistency is reached; it probably will not be necessary to use all of it.
  5. Simmer for 5 more minutes, and then serve.

Suggestion: Serve on a bed of white rice.
